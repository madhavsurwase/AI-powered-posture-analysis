# **App Name**: PosturePro

## Core Features:

- Video Input: Allows users to upload a video or use their webcam to record themselves performing squats or sitting at a desk.
- Pose Estimation: AI tool using MediaPipe to analyze the video feed and extract pose keypoints in real time.
- Posture Analysis: AI tool applying rule-based logic to identify instances of bad posture, such as knees over toes, slouching, or incorrect back angles.
- Real-Time Feedback: Highlights joints and displays real-time feedback messages to indicate whether the posture is correct or incorrect.
- Video Display: Displays the video feed with posture analysis results overlaid.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: #3B82F6 (a vibrant blue) to convey a sense of trust and technological advancement.
- Background color: #E5E7EB (a light grey) to provide a clean, unobtrusive backdrop that focuses user attention on the video and feedback.
- Accent color: #4ADE80 (a bright green) to highlight positive posture feedback and key interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font known for its readability and modern appearance.
- Use clear and intuitive icons to represent different posture states (good, bad, neutral).
- Design a clean and organized layout with the video feed prominently displayed, along with real-time feedback and controls.
